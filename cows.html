Our Cows

Meet Our Dairy Queens:
Holstein and Jersey

At MilkYatra Farmstead, we take pride in our diverse herd that graces the pastures with both the classic black and white patterns of the Holstein Freisians and the charming light brown hues of the Jerseys. While our majestic Holsteins are the larger of the bunch and champions in milk production, our dainty Jerseys may produce less but compensate with milk that's rich and creamy, just the way nature intended.

Cow Comfort

A content cow is our priority because a happy cow is a productive cow. We've designed a living environment that minimizes stress and maximizes comfort—ample space, constant access to fresh water and nutritious food, and shelters that shield our cows from the elements. Our belief is simple: care for cows leads to quality milk.

Comfortable Beds

We've invested in custom-designed bedding that provides each cow her own clean, dry space to rest and ruminate. Our cows enjoy up to 12 hours of rest daily, crucial for their well-being and milk yield.

Stress Free

YoCows do not like change and are very aware of their environment. We do our best to provide a comfortable place for them to rest, eat and drink at all times. Calm cows are healthier and produce more milk.

Open Spaces

Our barns are designed to promote free movement throughout the space. At any time, our cows can find a place to lay down, to eat, or a spot to drink water without competing with another animal.

Balanced Nutrition

Sustainable Diets for Thriving Cows

At MilkYatra, we blend tradition with innovation to create diets that respect the earth and fulfill our cows' nutritional needs. Our tailored feeding plans, developed by expert nutritionists, ensure our cows get the right balance of organic greens, grains, and minerals.

Efficient Nutrition, Exceptional Milk

Every bite our cows take is designed to boost their well-being and milk quality naturally. We believe in efficient nutrition - a way that enhances health without excesses and aligns with our eco-conscious values.

Green Energy, Healthier Herds

Our approach to energy is clear: use renewable, sustainably sourced feed to nourish our cows and protect our planet. MilkYatra is dedicated to delivering milk that's as pure and natural as the sustainable practices we stand for.

Grass-Fed, Grain-Enhanced

Balanced Grass-Grain Diets

At MilkYatra, we honor nature’s wisdom with our grass-rich feeding regimen. Our cows graze on lush pastures, receiving 80% of their diet from forages, imbuing their milk with the wholesome goodness of a grass-fed diet.

Harmony in Nutrition

Understanding the needs of both our consumers and our cows, we add a carefully measured 20% grain to their diet. This ensures our cows get the necessary energy to stay healthy and productive, complementing the natural diet with essential nutrients.

Vitality from the Ground Up

Grain isn't merely a feed; it's a vitality source that prevents energy deficits and supports the cows' overall well-being. This balance is MilkYatra’ commitment to sustainable farming and premium dairy products.

Healthy Cows, Healthy Future

Calves, The Heart of MilkYatra

At MilkYatra, we cherish our calves as the very essence of our farm's future. During those pivotal first weeks of life, we provide them with the utmost care and attention to ensure their well-being and growth.

From cozy living spaces to nourishing milk and grain, we prioritize every aspect of their care. Moreover, we believe in the importance of social interaction among peers, fostering a sense of community and companionship. At MilkYatra, our commitment is clear: to nurture thriving calves who embody our vision for a sustainable and compassionate future in agriculture.

Promoting Cow Well-Being

At MilkYatra, we prioritize the health of our cows above all else. Working closely with a nutritionist, we craft diets that meet every nutritional need of our milking cows, ensuring they stay healthy and strong.

Regular visits from our veterinarians help us maintain top-notch health standards, while our barns provide a stress-free environment for our cows to thrive. With open sides for ample airflow and access to outdoor patios, our modern barns offer comfort and stability in all seasons, ensuring our cows live their best lives at MilkYatra.

Housing at MilkYatra

Our barns provide a stress-free haven for our cows, ensuring stability and comfort in their daily lives. Engineered for optimal airflow, our modern dairy barns keep the air fresh and our cows content, with open sides and spacious doors facilitating circulation.

Additionally, many barns offer outdoor patios, allowing cows to bask in fair weather, while during harsh conditions, we can securely seal the barns to provide warmth and protection. We prioritize our cows' well-being by creating environments that cater to their needs and ensure their happiness.

MilkYatra Farmstead: A Family Legacy

We're more than just a farm—we're a family. And every cow, calf, and bottle of milk is a testament to our dedication to sustainable, compassionate dairy farming.
